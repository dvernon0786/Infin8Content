Direction 1: Professional & Efficient (Dense) RECOMMENDED

Maximum information density, compact spacing, efficient use of space. Best for power users and agencies managing many clients.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Layout Intuitiveness - Does the information hierarchy match your priorities?
  • Interaction Style - Does the interaction style fit your core experience?
  • Visual Weight - Does the visual density feel right for your brand?
  • Navigation Approach - Does the navigation pattern match user expectations?
  • Component Usage - How well do the components support your user journeys?
  • Brand Alignment - Does this direction support your emotional goals?
