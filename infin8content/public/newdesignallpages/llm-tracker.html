❓  FAQ

AI Visibility Tracking FAQ

What AI platforms do you track?
+
We track all major AI search platforms including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Claude, and Grok. All platforms are included in one price — no per-platform fees like other tools charge.
What metrics does the AI visibility tracker measure?
+
We track share of voice (percentage of AI responses mentioning your brand), direct mentions, citations (when AI links to your content), impressions weighted by search volume, and sentiment analysis. You can also see the actual AI responses that reference your brand.
Is AI visibility tracking included in my plan?
+
AI visibility tracking is included in all Infin8Content plans. Unlike competitors who charge $99–199/month for each AI platform separately, we include all platforms in your subscription at no extra cost.
How is this different from Ahrefs Brand Radar or other tools?
+
Most AI visibility tools charge per platform — Ahrefs Brand Radar charges separately for each AI index. Infin8Content includes all AI platforms in one dashboard, one price. Complete visibility without fragmented costs or surprise fees.
Can I white label the AI visibility reports?
+
Yes. Agencies can white label AI visibility reports with their own logo, brand colors, and custom domain — perfect for sharing branded AI visibility dashboards with clients during monthly reporting.
